HOW TO PLAY

Drive Quest is a challenging game designed to help players improve their understanding of driving principles and skills. By using questions from the MTO Driverâ€™s Handbook, it aims to prepare Canadians, especially those in Ontario, for their G1 test, boosting their confidence in the process.

Players engage with a 5x5 grid featuring question blocks in Drive Quest. To initiate the game, the first player selects a question square by clicking on it.

Easy

Medium

Hard

There are a total of 3 different question levels. White being the easiest and red being the hardest. The harder the question, the more points you can earn.

Players take turns answering various driving related questions until there are no more questions left in the grid.

Win the game and earn your license when you get more correct answers than your opponent!
